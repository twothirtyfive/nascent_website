About

Nascent Technologies LLC is a boutique consulting practice focused on blockchain technology and engineering. I work hands-on with founders and technical teams to turn ideas into shipped systems—prioritizing reliability, clear economics, and operational sanity. Expertise spans stablecoins, Cosmos SDK & IBC, EVM integrations, and end-to-end protocol delivery.
